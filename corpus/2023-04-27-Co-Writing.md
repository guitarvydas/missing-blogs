# 2023-04-27-Co-Writing
One of the things I learned the hard way when studying songwriting. 1+1=3 if you brainstorm. Pair-programming is heading in that direction, but, I think it ain't full-blown brainstorming yet.

Co-written songs are generally better than single-writer songs. I had my nose rubbed in it when I was exposed to "The Big Bang". 9 co-writers. I hated it at first, then analyzed it anyway. It's sooo much better because of the various influences.
