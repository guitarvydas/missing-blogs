# 2023-04-02-Projectional Editingconcurrency# Projectional Editing

Lisp is a syntax that allows for projectional editing.  

In pure lisp, there are only 2 kinds of things

- Atoms
- Lists.

The "problem" with Lisp syntax is that it is burdened with Lisp meaning ("semantics").

Remove the meaning from Lisp syntax, and you get a pure syntax:
- Atoms
- Lists
- Separators.

I argue that this stripped-down syntax is the basis of projectional editing.
