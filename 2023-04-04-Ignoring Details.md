# 2023-04-04-Ignoring DetailsThe UNIX philosophy of "do one thing well" is about ignoring stuff that doesn't apply. 

Filter. 

Our universe is multi-dimensional. Our bodies can only perceive 4 dimensions (x/y/z/t). PROLOG can only express 2 dimensions. Mathematics (+ Greek) can only express 2 dimensions. Mathematics is a result of forcing ourselves to express things in 2 dimensions - clay tables (x/y). It is NOT POSSIBLE to have a unified expression of EVERYTHING in a 2D notation. The best we can hope for is many 2D expressions of bits-of-detail. 

The idea of "one language to rule them all" is laughable.
