# 2023-08-26-Design, Engineering, Production EngineeringCreation of solutions to Real problems (UX) requires, at least
1. Design
2. Engineering
3. Production Engineering (Optimization).

Deep type checking addresses only a tiny sliver of the problem (DX for Optimization) yet is worshipped as The Silver Bullet that will do away with the need for Iteration and Creativity (ad-hoc combination of ideas) to create Real Solutions.  50+ years later, Reality shows that this is not the case, and, that deep type checking itself is designed through iteration and ad-hocery.

Design and Engineering have been abandoned and relegated to relatively feeble approaches, such as Agile.
