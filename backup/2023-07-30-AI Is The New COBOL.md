# 2023-07-30-AI Is The New COBOLAI is the new COBOL.

COBOL was invented to provide a more readable syntax to laypeople for writing programs.

Today, ChatGPT allows laypeople to write instructions using ambiguous terms. The computer takes action or creates small programs based on its interpretation of the ambiguous instructions.
