## Make Better Use of Silicon
Small steps.  

Step 1: get rid of bloatware.  Deprecate Operating Systems (Linux, Windows, MacOS, etc.).

Note: the ground rules have changed.  In 1950, CPUs were expensive and scarce.  The idea of lathering them with Operating Systems made sense.

in 2023: We can have bowls full of Raspberry PIs.  We don't need to time-share CPUs anymore.  A bunch of rPIs are naturally concurrent.  There is no need to lather them with synchronous paradigm software, then to regain asynchronocity by lathering the latherings with self-flagellation like processes, thread safety, semaphores, etc, etc.
