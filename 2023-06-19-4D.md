# 2023-06-19-4D# 4D
Magnetic fields are actually 4D phenomena (x/y/z/t).  

We use equations to think about fields by reducing fields to 2D so that we may scribble our thoughts down onto clay tablets and papyrus.  

There is this new medium - called "computers" - that might let us break free of 2D-only expression of 4D phenomena, using, say, 3D (x/y/t) while expressing the 4th D (z) using a trick that humanity invented, called "perspective".  

Fields might be more-than-4D phenomena but our brains can only perceive 4 dimensions at this time.
