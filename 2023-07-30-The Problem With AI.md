# 2023-07-30-The Problem With AI
The problem with AI is: intelligence.

We want the machine to be our slave.  We want it to do exactly what we wish it to do.

We don't want the machine to have free will.

ChatGPT shows one of the problems of artificial intelligence. It makes stuff up instead of unambiguously interpreting our instructions. This is called "hallucination" by researchers.  Normal humans refer to this as "bullsh_t".

Ambiguous instructions should not lead to ambiguous results. Ambiguous instructions certainly should not lead to invention.
