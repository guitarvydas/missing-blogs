# 2023-08-03-Trickle-Down Economics of Software Development
- trickle down economics doesn't work
	- examples: Credit Default Swaps
		- people who gain a lot of Money, don't trickle that Money down to the masses
		- instead, they continue doing what they've been doing - gambling their Money on shakier and shakier investments, and, don't cash out and donate their winnings to the masses
 - same in S/W - "trickle down" doesn't work
	 - Type Checking disciples don't stop and cash out their research to the masses, they continue building higher towers of Type Checking
	- Type-Checking theory begets more complexity, not greater *understandability* for "normal" programmers
	- this is the "ivory tower"
